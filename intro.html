Welcome to Spot the Phishing

About This Quiz

This interactive quiz will help you learn how to identify phishing emails. You'll be presented with various email scenarios and asked to determine if they're legitimate or phishing attempts.

How to Play

  • You'll see emails that look like they're from Microsoft Outlook
  • Click on underlined elements to inspect them for suspicious details
  • Look for clues in sender addresses, links, and attachments
  • Choose "Phishing" or "Legitimate" for each email
  • Get immediate feedback and explanations for your choices

What to Look For

Common Phishing Signs:

  • Suspicious sender email addresses (e.g., micros0ft.com instead of microsoft.com)
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Unexpected attachments, especially .exe files
  • Links that don't match the sender's domain
  • Requests for personal information or account verification
